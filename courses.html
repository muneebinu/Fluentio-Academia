Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before enrolling

Are the classes live or recorded? +
All classes are conducted live via Zoom or Google Meet. This is intentional — real speaking confidence only comes from live practice with a real instructor. Recordings are shared after each session.
I'm a complete beginner. Is this suitable for me? +
Absolutely. The Standard Plan is designed specifically for beginners. We start from everyday spoken English and build you up to confident, natural communication — no heavy grammar theory required.
How long is each course? +
The Standard Plan is a 30–45 day program. The Premium Plan is 45–60 days depending on your goals. You'll feel a noticeable difference within the first two weeks.
What's the difference between Standard and Premium? +
Standard is group-based and ideal for general fluency. Premium adds 1-on-1 sessions, personal feedback, interview preparation, and weekly progress tracking — perfect for serious learners with specific goals like job interviews or IELTS.
How do I pay? Is it safe? +
Payment can be made via EasyPaisa, JazzCash, or bank transfer. Contact us on WhatsApp and we'll guide you through enrollment in minutes.
